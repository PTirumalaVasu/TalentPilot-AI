- (technique) started Job to Be Done
- (idea by coach) JTBD (employee): close my specific skill gap fast, with a next-step handed to me — not 'manage my learning'
- (idea by coach) JTBD (HR): prove in one glance the org can do X (staffing/audit/succession) without emailing five spreadsheet owners
- (idea by user) HR Manager JTBD: needs centralized dashboard to assign skills, monitor progress, and quickly identify project-ready resources (replacing spreadsheet tracking/reporting)
- (idea by user) Employee JTBD: needs AI assistant to instantly find relevant topics within learning videos, resume from last watched position, complete learning efficiently without manual searching
- (idea by coach) Skill-gap-to-project matcher: HR specifies required skills for a project, system surfaces who has them AND who is X% there and could close gap in time
- (idea by coach) Transcript-level semantic video index: employee asks a question in natural language, jumps straight to the exact timestamp instead of scrubbing
- (idea by user) Sharpest pain: employees manually maintain Excel sheets for learning roadmap, skill progress, updates -> manual effort + inconsistent tracking
- (idea by coach) Auto-capture progress: system infers roadmap/skill progress from actual video watch %, quiz results etc. instead of employee manually logging it in Excel
- (technique) started SCAMPER Method
- (idea by user) Eliminate confirmed: zero manual entry by employee — their only job is to start/resume learning at their convenience, system handles everything else
- (idea by user) Two-layer roadmap: HR sets the must-do skills/courses; Employee uses AI assistant to fetch relevant learning videos and start/resume learning within that
- (idea by coach) Proactive nudge: system pings employee to resume ('you paused 3 days ago, 8 min left') since nobody manually chases progress anymore
- (decision by user) Scope boundary: AI assistant only identifies relevant video tutorials/documents/websites based on assigned skills and tracks video progress — no proactive skill-gap detection
- (decision by user) Progress tracking applies to videos only — documents/websites are recommended content with no progress tracking needed
- (technique) started Morphological Analysis
- (decision by user) Only two roles: HR (assigns skills, org-wide view) and Employee (learns/tracks own progress) — no separate Manager/Team Lead role
- (decision by user) Project-ready resource identification is manual — HR looks at the dashboard and decides, no automated skill-gap-to-project matcher needed (earlier coach idea shelved for MVP)
- (idea by user) HR dashboard per-employee columns: name, skills, sub-skills, start date, estimated end date, actual end date, status, blocker
- (decision by user) Blocker column removed from HR dashboard scope for now
- (technique) started Cross-Pollination
- (idea by user) Adopt Netflix/Spotify pattern: 'continue watching' row + resume-exactly-where-left-off (no auto-recommendations)
- (decision by user) MoSCoW confirmed. MUST(MVP): HR dashboard (employee/skills/sub-skills/dates/status), HR assigns must-do skills, AI assistant content search (video/doc/website) by assigned skill, auto video progress tracking, continue-watching+resume-exact-position. SHOULD(fast-follow): proactive resume nudges, transcript-level semantic timestamp search. WONT(this time): automated skill-gap-to-project matcher, blocker column, manager/team-lead role, doc/website progress tracking, post-completion recommendations, AI substituting manager 1:1s/proactive gap-flagging
- (insight) Core synthesis: MVP reduces to 3 data flows — HR writes assignments in, platform auto-reads video-consumption signals, HR reads dashboard built from those signals. No manual employee entry, no fuzzy AI judgment calls (gap-matching/mentoring rejected) — a narrow, scalable, enterprise-buildable slice. MoSCoW confirmed for build scope.

# Business Goals & Objectives

> Strategic goals and measurable objectives for TalentPilot-AI

**Document:** Trigger Map - Business Goals
**Created:** 2026-07-08
**Status:** COMPLETE

---

## Vision

**Replace SAILS HR's spreadsheet-dependent skill tracking with a dashboard built on automatically-captured signal, not self-reported status — so HR can open it and simply trust what it says, without chasing anyone for an update.**

---

## Business Objectives

### ⭐ PRIMARY GOAL: Prove the Evidence-Pipeline Hypothesis (THE ENGINE)

- **Statement:** Cheaply and quickly prove that automated, evidence-based learning signals meaningfully outperform manual self-reporting for skill readiness, before SAILS commits to a larger investment in either direction.
- **Metric:** Self-reported skill data staleness rate
- **Target:** Below 5%
- **Timeline:** Within 60 days of launch (by 11 September 2026)
- **Impact:** This is the entire reason the pilot exists — every other objective is a downstream consequence of this hypothesis holding true. If auto-capture doesn't meaningfully beat self-reporting, nothing else about the product matters.

---

### 🚀 EARN HR'S TRUST (Driven by the Evidence Pipeline)

**Objective 1: Become HR's primary source of truth**
- **Statement:** HR uses TalentPilot-AI as the primary reference during talent reviews, with minimal-to-no reliance on the legacy spreadsheet.
- **Metric:** Dashboard usage analytics + direct stakeholder feedback (not self-report alone)
- **Target:** Confirmed primary-source status at the 60-day checkpoint
- **Timeline:** 60 days post-launch (~11 September 2026)

**Objective 2: Launch on the hard deadline**
- **Statement:** Ship the MVP (HR assignment flow, AI content discovery, video progress tracking, continue-watching/resume) on schedule.
- **Metric:** Feature-complete, in-use system
- **Target:** Live for both HR and Employee roles
- **Timeline:** 13 July 2026 (hard line, not movable)

**Objective 3: Make trust legible, not just present**
- **Statement:** Every data point on the dashboard carries an explicit provenance label (verified / self-reported / needs attention) with drill-down reasoning, so HR's trust is based on visible evidence, not a uniform-looking grid.
- **Metric:** Presence and usage of provenance labels and the "Needs Attention" filter in real HR sessions
- **Target:** HR filters to "Needs Attention" rather than scanning every row out of habit
- **Timeline:** Present at launch, validated by usage pattern within 60 days

---

### 🌟 ELIMINATE THE SELF-REPORT CHORE (Real-World Benefit for Employees)

**Note:** This tier is the direct benefit TalentPilot-AI creates FOR employees — the chore they've been quietly avoiding disappears without anyone asking them to try harder.

**Objective 4: Zero manual progress logging for video-based learning**
- **Statement:** Employees never re-type or self-report status for any assigned video content — the resume/continue-watching mechanic and the trust signal HR sees are the same write, with no separate reporting step.
- **Metric:** % of video-based assignments requiring zero manual employee input
- **Target:** 100% of video assignments auto-captured
- **Timeline:** At launch (13 July 2026)
- **Benefit to Employees:** Removes an incentive-free chore entirely, replacing it with a personal payoff (resume exactly where they left off) they'd want anyway.

**Objective 5: Fast, relevant content discovery**
- **Statement:** Employees find the right learning content quickly once HR assigns a skill, via AI-surfaced video/doc/web content matched to the assignment.
- **Metric:** Time from assignment to first content engagement
- **Target:** Employees start relevant content without needing to search for it themselves
- **Timeline:** At launch (13 July 2026)
- **Benefit to Employees:** Removes the "what am I even supposed to watch" friction on top of removing the reporting chore.

---

## The Flywheel: How Goals Connect

**THE ENGINE (Priority #1):**
- Prove the evidence-pipeline hypothesis — staleness below 5% within 60 days
- Timeline: 60 days from 13 July 2026 launch
- This is a single technical bet (auto-captured video watch-% as trust signal) that, if proven, justifies every other investment in this space
- It creates the flywheel that drives ALL other objectives — without proof here, "earning HR's trust" and "eliminating the chore" are just features nobody has reason to keep funding

**Earn HR's Trust (Priority #2):**
- Driven BY the evidence pipeline succeeding
- Primary-source-of-truth status, on-time launch, legible provenance labeling
- Timeline: Launch 13 July 2026, confirmed by 60-day checkpoint
- Focus: Converting proven technical signal into HR's actual daily behavior change (opening TalentPilot-AI instead of the spreadsheet)

**Eliminate the Self-Report Chore (Priority #3):**
- Real-world benefit FOR employees, not a company metric
- Zero manual logging for video content, fast content discovery
- Timeline: Present at launch
- **Key benefit:** Employees get a Netflix/Spotify-style resume experience for free, as a side effect of the same signal that solves HR's trust problem — nobody has to ask them to do more work to get it.

## Success Metrics Alignment

### How Trigger Map Connects to Objectives (Properly Prioritized):

**⭐ PRIMARY: Rita Trusts the Dashboard Enough to Act On It → Achieves:**
- ✅ **Staleness below 5% within 60 days** (THE ENGINE — Rita's daily behavior is the direct measurement instrument)
- ✅ Rita stops chasing employees for status updates
- ✅ Rita makes staffing/readiness calls with confidence instead of suspicion
- ✅ Validates the root-cause hypothesis (self-reporting, not spreadsheet format, was the real pain)
- **Timeline: 60 days post-launch**
- **This drives ALL other objectives**

**🚀 SECONDARY: Rita's Trust Becomes Habitual Primary-Source Usage → Achieves:**
- ✅ Dashboard usage overtakes spreadsheet reliance (Rita opens TalentPilot-AI instead of Excel)
- ✅ On-time launch objective (Rita has something to trust by 13 July)
- ✅ Provenance-labeled trust becomes visible, not assumed (Rita uses "Needs Attention" filter)
- **Timeline: 60 days post-launch**

**🌟 TERTIARY: Casey's Chore-Free Experience Reinforces the Signal → Achieves:**
- ✅ Zero manual logging (Casey's participation in the system is passive, so the signal keeps flowing without Rita having to enforce compliance)
- ✅ Fast content discovery (Casey engages with assigned content instead of ignoring it)
- ✅ A virtuous loop: Casey's honest, effortless engagement is precisely what keeps Rita's dashboard trustworthy
- **Timeline: Present at launch**
- **Benefit: Casey experiences a consumer-app-quality resume feature; SAILS gets a self-sustaining evidence pipeline as a byproduct**

**The Trigger Map IS the Strategic Foundation - And Prioritization Matters**

The product must prove the evidence pipeline works → Rita starts trusting the dashboard → that trust becomes daily primary-source behavior → Casey's frictionless participation is what keeps the signal honest and the whole loop running without anyone having to be chased.

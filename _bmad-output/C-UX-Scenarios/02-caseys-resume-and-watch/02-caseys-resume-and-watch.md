# 02: Casey's Resume & Watch

**Project:** TalentPilot-AI  
**Created:** 2026-07-08  
**Method:** Whiteport Design Studio (WDS)

---

## Transaction (Q1)

**What this scenario covers:**
Casey receives an assigned skill, discovers AI-surfaced content, watches a video partway through, closes the tab mid-video, returns days later, and resumes at exactly the spot they left off — with zero manual reporting and zero lost progress.

---

## Business Goal (Q2)

**Goal:** POC Hypothesis Test  
**Objective:** Test whether frictionless resume + auto-capture works as designed. Does Casey's passive participation actually generate the verified signal Rita depends on?

---

## User & Situation (Q3)

**Persona:** Casey the Continuer (SECONDARY 💼)  
**Situation:** Casey is an individual contributor at SAILS Software who receives an assigned skill from Rita in TalentPilot-AI.

---

## Driving Forces (Q4)

**Hope:** See the assigned skill with relevant content ready to watch, start the video, and know that if they pause or close the tab, they can pick up exactly where they left off next time — like Netflix.

**Worry:** Losing their place in the video and having to re-scrub through content they already watched, or the system failing to save their progress and showing they got no credit for watching.

---

## Device & Starting Point (Q5 + Q6)

**Device:** Laptop (desktop equivalent)  
**Entry:** Casey receives a Slack notification or email from Rita: "Data Visualization Fundamentals assigned to you in TalentPilot-AI." Casey clicks the link or opens the TalentPilot-AI bookmark and sees the assignment card.

---

## Best Outcome (Q7)

**User Success:**
Casey watches the video to 14:32 (out of 28 min), closes the tab, returns 3 days later, opens TalentPilot-AI, clicks "Continue Watching," and resumes at exactly 14:32 — with no manual remembering, re-scrubbing, or status-reporting step. Zero friction.

**Business Success:**
The watch-position write that lets Casey resume also updates Rita's dashboard to show "Verified · 51% watched, 3 days ago" — Casey's passive participation generates trustworthy signal for Rita, and Casey's convenience is the mechanism keeping that signal honest.

---

## Shortest Path (Q8)

1. **Content Discovery** — Casey sees the assigned skill with human-approved, AI-surfaced content recommendation already pre-selected (not a search box). Thumbnail + duration visible. Casey clicks play.
2. **Resume/Continue Watching** — Casey watches the video from the start to 14:32, then closes the tab (interrupted). Watch-position is captured. 3 days later, Casey returns to TalentPilot-AI and sees a "Continue Watching" card showing the skill, progress bar at 51%, and "14 min remaining." Casey clicks to resume at 14:32. ✓

### JavaScript

```javascript
function toggleUserMenu() {
  document.querySelector('[data-dropdown]').classList.toggle('hidden');
}
document.getElementById('skills-dashboard-user-menu').querySelector('button')
  .addEventListener('click', toggleUserMenu);
```

## Common Issues & Fixes

### Issue: Dropdown doesn't hide on outside click
**Symptom**: Dropdown stays open when clicking elsewhere
**Cause**: No document-level click listener to close it
**Fix**: Add later in Section 6 (Interactions & Polish) alongside the modal's click-outside handling — not required for this section's acceptance criteria.

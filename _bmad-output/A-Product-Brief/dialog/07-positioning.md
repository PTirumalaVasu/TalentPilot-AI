## Final Positioning Statement

> For SAILS HR admins who need trustworthy, low-effort visibility into employee skill readiness, TalentPilot-AI is an internal skill-assignment and tracking dashboard that replaces self-reported spreadsheet status with automatically-captured video-progress signal. Unlike the current spreadsheet — or a heavyweight LMS/talent-intelligence suite that would be disproportionate for testing this one hypothesis — TalentPilot-AI is narrow, fast to stand up, and honest about what it can and can't verify: video progress is auto-verified, everything else is clearly labeled as self-reported.

**Components:**

- **Target Customer:** SAILS HR/L&D admins (primary); Employees (secondary, in-product user)
- **Their Need:** Trustworthy, low-effort skill-readiness visibility without chasing self-reported status updates
- **Product Category:** Internal skill-assignment and tracking dashboard (narrow scope — not LMS/LXP, not talent-intelligence suite)
- **Key Benefit:** A dashboard HR can open and simply trust
- **Alternatives:** The current shared Excel spreadsheet (sole real incumbent); buying a heavyweight platform (Cornerstone/Degreed/LinkedIn Learning) was explicitly considered and rejected as disproportionate
- **Differentiator:** Fully automatic video-progress capture (not self-reported) + honest "mixed trust, clearly labeled" data model — verified vs. self-reported vs. needs-attention, rather than a uniform, unverifiable grid

**Strategic Rationale:**
Because this is a solo-driven internal pilot with no procurement process, positioning doesn't need to compete against named commercial vendors — it only needs to beat the spreadsheet on trust and effort, which the auto-capture + labeling mechanism does directly. Framing the build-vs-buy rejection explicitly (rather than ignoring it) also pre-empts the most obvious internal question ("why not just buy something?") before anyone asks it.
